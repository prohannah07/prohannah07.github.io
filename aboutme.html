You can call me Hannah. I am currently a 4th year Software Engineering Major and Informatics Minor at UCI. A little background about me, I am a Filipino immigrant and we moved to the United States about 7 years ago. Back then, I didn't know what moving would have meant for me but now, I can for sure say that it changed my life and opened up a lot of opportunities that I otherwise wouldn't have gotten. I am very thankful for my parents for making this possible for me. Right now, I am still trying to figure out what I truly want to do but as of late, I think I have been leaning more towards Informatics and all things related to it. I've been realizing that I really enjoy front-end development and designing for the user. I am hoping to be in a career that is like that in the future.
